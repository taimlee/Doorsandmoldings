Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Solid Doors & Moldings ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at soliddoorsandmoldings.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you use our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you fill out our contact form or request a quote, you may provide:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Project details and specifications
  • Business name and role
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, standard web server logs may capture basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. We do not use this information to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • Communicate about your project
  • Send follow-up information you have requested

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We will not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Email and Forms

Contact and quote forms on this site submit data to FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form processing service. Your submission is transmitted to us via email. FormSubmit's privacy policy is available at formsubmit.co. We retain form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or analytics services. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through our website. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. For sensitive inquiries, please use our contact form directly.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us by contacting us through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
