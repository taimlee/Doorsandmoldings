Wood Species
Questions about materials, sourcing, and selection.

We offer over 20 domestic and imported species including White Oak, Red Oak, Walnut, Cherry, Mahogany, Maple, Alder, Poplar, Douglas Fir, and more. We can source specialty species on request. All wood is kiln-dried to 6–8% moisture content before milling.

A solid wood door is constructed entirely from real wood — either a single thick piece or multiple pieces laminated for stability. A solid core door has a wood or composite core (MDF or particleboard) wrapped in a wood veneer. We make only solid wood doors. Solid core doors are a budget alternative but will not hold the same character, weight, or lifespan.

Yes. We hold FSC Chain-of-Custody certification. All hardwoods we purchase are sourced from FSC-certified forests. We can provide FSC project documentation for LEED certification submissions.

Custom Sizing
Non-standard dimensions, historic renovation, and special configurations.

Yes — all our doors can be made to any custom size. We commonly produce oversized entry doors (8'×4', 10'×5', and larger), low-clearance interior doors, and any width/height combination needed for historic renovation or custom architecture.

For a replacement, measure the existing door's height, width, and thickness — not the opening. Standard US interior doors are 80" tall and 24"–36" wide in 2" increments, 1-3/8" thick. Exterior doors are typically 1-3/4" thick. If you're unsure, contact us and we'll walk you through it.

Finishes
Paint, stain, and coating options.

We offer raw/unfinished, shop-primed, painted (any Benjamin Moore or Sherwin-Williams color), and stained/topcoated in dozens of standard stain colors. Custom finish matching is available. All our applied finishes are low-VOC and CARB Phase 2 compliant.

Fire-Rated Doors
UL listings, ratings, and code compliance.

Yes. We manufacture UL-listed fire-rated solid wood doors in 20, 45, 60, and 90-minute ratings. These are code-compliant for commercial and residential applications and available in our standard species and finishes.

Warranty
What we cover and for how long.

We offer a workmanship warranty on all doors and moldings — covering joinery, panel movement, and structural integrity. Finish and hardware warranties are 5 years. Warranty claims are handled directly by our team; no third-party runaround.

Installation
Professional installation options and guidance.

We do not directly employ installers, but we maintain a network of certified installation partners in most US markets. We can coordinate installation through our project management team. For local Nashville projects, we can often recommend our preferred installers directly.
