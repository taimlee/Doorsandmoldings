Fire Ratings

Four Ratings. One Standard of Craft.

20
Minute Rating

Corridor and exit access doors. Common in residential and light commercial applications.

45
Minute Rating

Exit enclosures, stairwells, and most commercial interior openings. The most commonly specified commercial rating.

60
Minute Rating

Openings in 2-hour fire-rated walls. Required for many hotel corridor and suite entry applications.

90
Minute Rating

High-rise and heavy commercial applications. 3-hour fire wall openings in industrial and institutional buildings.
