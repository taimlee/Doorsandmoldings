Custom Capabilities

What We Can Build for You

Oversized & Grand Entry

Doors up to 16' tall and any width. Engineered for stability with internal lamination and steel reinforcement where needed.

Arched & Radius Tops

Full radius, segmental, Gothic, and elliptical tops. We bend-laminate the arch rail for precise, repeatable radius.

Carved & Routed Panels

Raised, carved, and CNC-routed panel designs. Monograms, architectural motifs, and custom relief work.

Matched Grain / Book-Match

We can flitch-match or book-match faces across single or paired doors for a seamless, continuous grain pattern.

Pivot Doors

Floor-to-ceiling pivot configurations on concealed pivot hardware. A modern entry statement unlike anything hung on standard hinges.

Specialty Species

Teak, Sapele, Wenge, Ipe, White Ash, Figured Maple, Burl, and more. We source on request from our trusted lumber partners.

Historic Replication

We recreate historic door profiles from photographs, profiles, or original templates. Common in preservation and renovation projects.

Commercial Scale

Custom doors at volume — 10 units or 10,000. We've built custom door packages for hotels, corporate campuses, and multi-family developments.

Multi-Panel Systems

Folding, sliding, stacking, and pocketing multi-panel door systems. Operable walls that create or erase space.

Our Process

How a Custom Door Gets Built

01
Discovery Call
We talk through your vision — opening size, architectural style, species preference, hardware, and timeline.
02
Shop Drawings
Our design team produces detailed shop drawings and a wood species / finish specification. You approve before we cut.
03
Production
Lumber is selected, cut, assembled, and finished in our Nashville facility. Progress photos at key milestones.
04
Delivery & Install
Custom crated and shipped nationwide. White-glove and installation coordination available in most markets.
