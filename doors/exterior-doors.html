Exterior-Grade Construction Throughout

Every exterior door we make is built with weather performance in mind — from species selection to glue choice to the finish system.

Recommended species for exterior use: Mahogany, Douglas Fir, White Oak, Teak, and Western Red Cedar. We advise against Poplar or Maple for unprotected exterior applications.
