Accessibility

Last updated: April 2026

Solid Doors & Moldings is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We are working to ensure our site conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Our Commitment

We believe that all users deserve equal access to information and functionality. As we continue to improve our website, we are focused on:

  • Providing sufficient color contrast for readability
  • Supporting keyboard navigation throughout the site
  • Including descriptive text alternatives for images
  • Using clear, consistent page structure and headings
  • Ensuring form fields are properly labeled
  • Making all interactive elements operable without a mouse

Known Limitations

We are actively working to improve our site's accessibility. Some areas that are in progress include:

  • Placeholder image descriptions — images served from external sources may have limited alternative text
  • Some older embedded content may not yet meet full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to work with common assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • Browser zoom and text resizing
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • High contrast display modes

Need Assistance?

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our website, or if you need information in an alternative format, please contact us. We will do our best to respond within two business days.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our site. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know so we can address it. Your input helps us serve all customers better.
