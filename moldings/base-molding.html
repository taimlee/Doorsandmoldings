Built to Hold Paint — and Take a Hit

Poplar is our standard paint-grade species — it mills cleanly, holds paint without bleeding, and resists impact. For stained applications, we recommend White Oak, Cherry, or Walnut.

Base shoe and base cap are available to match any profile — sold separately or as part of a complete 3-piece system.
