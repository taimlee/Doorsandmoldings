Applications

What Panel Molding Can Do

Wall panel grid — formal dining, painted
Wall Panel Grid

Grid of rectangular panels applied to a full wall. Creates the look of full raised-panel millwork at a fraction of the cost.

Picture frame molding — gallery wall, formal room
Picture Frame Molding

Smaller frames applied at art-hanging height to define a gallery zone. Elegant and practical.

Shadow box panels — contemporary bedroom feature wall
Shadow Box Panel

Flat panel molding applied in a grid to create depth and geometry — a modern alternative to traditional raised panels.

Wainscoting cap with panel below — hallway
Below Chair Rail Panel

Applied below the chair rail in combination with wainscoting — creates the effect of full raised-panel woodwork.

Coffered ceiling panel molding — library beams
Coffered Ceiling Panels

Panel molding applied to ceilings between beams to create coffers — adds drama and visual depth above.

Staircase wall panel molding — angled installation
Stair Wall Panels

Panel molding along a staircase wall — angled cuts that follow the rake of the stair. We pre-cut angled pieces on request.
