Placement Guide

Where to Mount Your Chair Rail

Height depends on room use and ceiling height. Here are our standard recommendations.

32"
Dining Rooms
Traditional height — aligns with the top rail of most dining chairs.
36"
Hallways & Entry
Slightly higher to clear console tables and hall furniture.
1/3 Height
General Rule
One third of ceiling height — scales proportionally with the room.
